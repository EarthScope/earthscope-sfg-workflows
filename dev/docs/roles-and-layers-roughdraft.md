## 1. Scope

Network is a collection of GNSS-A transponder arrays with unique id's (i.e. NCC1) and locations.

Station is a unique location with 1 or more transponders.

Campaign is a multi-day span of GNSS-A surveys for a given station.

Survey is a multi hour span of continuious GNSS-A ping reply sequences.
